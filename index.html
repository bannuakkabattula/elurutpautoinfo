How It Works – For Passengers

Quick, Safe & Verified Auto Travel in Eluru

Step 1: See the ID Card

Every auto driver will have a digital ID card with a QR code and photo displayed.

Step 2: Scan the QR Code

Use your mobile camera or app to scan the QR code on the ID card.

Step 3: Check Driver Details

You’ll instantly see the driver’s: Name Photo Vehicle number Police verification status

Step 4: Verify & Travel Safely

If everything looks good, start your ride with confidence. You know the driver is officially registered and verified.
